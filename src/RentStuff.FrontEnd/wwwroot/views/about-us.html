How our algorithms work?

When you enter a location, we provide you with a list of properties (hostels, houses, apartments, hotels or guest houses) available for rent starting from properties available nearest to the search location. And we use Google Geocoding service integrated with our tested algorithms to accomplish that. So whether it is a house for rent in Bahria Town or hostel in Islamabad, you can post your property or saerch for one anywhere in Pakistan.

How to find suitable hostels or houses on rent for accommodation?

How to find hostels or houses & apartments on rent in a location of your choosing? Sure you can search for Hostels in Islamabad or Hostels in Rawalpindi, But how about making it more granular? Like hostels in F-7 Islamabad or House for rent in Bahria Town Rawalpindi?
So we came up with the idea of Zarqoon; to help people in finding hostels in Islamabad & Rawalpindi, plus apartments and houses for rent, so that everyone is able to find accommodation in a specific area and they dont have to go through the headache of trying to locate one in an entire city.
